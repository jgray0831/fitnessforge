Welcome!

Fitness Forge is a web and mobile application that gives users the abilities to track, monitor, excel, and share their fitness progression with little to no effort. Fitness Forge is intended for all audiences, while still focusing on a clear cut path to a healthier lifestyle. With health being the end goal, Fitness Forge will support several different methods of exercise and will promote the experimentation of new, fun, and exciting “workouts”. The application will promote a sense of accomplishment and self-worth that will carry the users to work harder and to reach larger goals! We know that nearly 58% of all smartphone users have downloaded a fitness app, but most users have never actively used the app. Fitness Forge looks to reinvent the wheel and provide a set of quality of life enhancements that will motivate users, reward dedicated members, and keep the general population returning again and again.
